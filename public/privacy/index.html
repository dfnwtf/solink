1. Our Commitment to Privacy

SOLink is built with privacy at its core. We believe your communications should remain private, and we've designed our system to ensure that even we cannot access the content of your messages.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Wallet Information (Public)

  • Your public wallet address (used as your identity)
  • Optional: Your chosen nickname (@username)

2.2 Encrypted Messages

  • Messages are encrypted client-side using NaCl (XSalsa20-Poly1305) before transmission
  • We store only encrypted ciphertext â€” we cannot decrypt or read your messages
  • Message metadata: sender address, recipient address, timestamp

2.3 Push Notification Data

  • You can disable push notifications at any time in Settings

2.4 Automatically Collected

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Message Delivery: Route encrypted messages between wallets
  • Notifications: Send push notifications for new messages (if enabled)
  • Service Operation: Maintain and improve the Service
  • Security: Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and fraud

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's global edge network, which provides:

  • Enterprise-grade security and DDoS protection
  • Data encryption at rest and in transit
  • SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and other security certifications

Encrypted messages are stored temporarily for delivery. Once delivered, messages are removed from our servers. Your local message history is stored only on your device.
